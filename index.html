Rules of the Game:

1 - The active player clicks in the Roll Dice button. A number between 1 and 6 will be rolled.

2 - If you roll a 1, it will change to the other player to play and your current score will become 0. If you rolled a number different than 1,you add the rolled number to your current score and you can choose between Holding the score and Rolling again.

3 - If you decide to roll again, you will acumulate more points at your current scores as long as you roll more than 1.

4 - If you decide to Hold, you current score will be added to your total score, your current score will become 0, and the active player will be switched to the other player.

5 - The first player that gets 50 points, 100 points or 150 points to the Total score after Holding, depending on the mode you are playing, wins the game.

6 - Click in the New game button if you want to restart the game. Click in the different modes buttons to switch modes.

7 - Have fun!!!
